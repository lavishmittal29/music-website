About Me

Hello, I'm Lavish Mittal, a passionate musician dedicated to creating powerful and emotive musical experiences. With over 10 years of musical training, I've mastered multiple instruments and developed a unique sound that blends classical techniques with modern innovations.

My musical journey began at the age of 7 when I first learned to play the dholak and piano, and I've been in love with creating music ever since. I've performed at various venues across the country and collaborated with talented artists from diverse musical backgrounds.

I believe music has the power to touch souls, transcend boundaries, and create meaningful connections between people. Through my performances and compositions, I strive to create moments of beauty, reflection, and joy.

Instruments I Play

Dholak

The dholak was my first instrument, which I started playing at age 7. This traditional Indian drum has been central to my musical foundation, allowing me to develop a deep understanding of rhythm and percussion. I've incorporated various regional playing styles, from Punjab to Rajasthan, into my technique.

Octapad

I incorporated the Octapad into my musical journey, utilizing its versatility to blend traditional percussion with modern electronic beats. My approach combines dynamic finger drumming techniques with customized sound modulation, allowing me to create rhythmic patterns that adapt to various genres, from classical fusion to contemporary music. The Octapad has become an integral part of my performances, adding depth, precision, and innovation to my compositions.

DHOL

I mastered the Indian instrument 'Dhol,' blending traditional rhythmic patterns with modern influences to produce dynamic beats suited for various musical styles, from folk to fusion. My approach combines intricate techniques with contemporary adaptations, allowing me to craft powerful and energetic rhythms that enhance any musical composition. The Dhol plays a significant role in my performances, adding depth and vibrancy to my music.
